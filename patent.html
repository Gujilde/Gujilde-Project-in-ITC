Single body multi-tool device

US7036952B2



A multi-tool gadget with a single body and multiple compartments in its housing.
Various hand tools, such as a hatchet, a digging tool, a flashlight, a knife, and a saw,
are mounted in these compartments, any of which can be used independently without interfering
with the operation and/or use of another tool. This multi-tool gadget is concerned with hand tools
in general, and in particular with hand tools used in the outdoors, such as while camping.
