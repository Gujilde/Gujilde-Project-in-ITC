A Multi-tool Lighter with LED Light, ALL in ONE



These multi-tool functions include a LED light and a lighter in one.
It's consist of a knife, saw, scissor from the right side. On the other side,
it has a can opener, flat, and philips screwdriver which is very convenient to
use for simple tasks and repairs. To the right bottom of the multi-tool, it has
a USB port near to the LED light so it is rechargeable which is good to use at
night either outside or inside of your home. The Sekyu is a quality, durable,
and stylish multi-tool that is made from only the highest quality stainless steel
and hard plastic. With proper care and use, it will provide you with years of
enjoyment and convenience in your life.
